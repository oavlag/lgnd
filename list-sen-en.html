WHAT TO PACK?

Follow the Senderista Packing List

1. What should the backpack be like?

  • Your backpack should have a capacity of approximately 45 liters / Example: Amazon link
  • The total weight of the backpack should be approximately 14kg (31 lbs), including tent, sleeping bag, clothes and water
  • Bring a waterproof cover for the backpack (plastic bag or rain cover)
  • Your backpack is individual
  • Keep documents protected from water (Ziplock bags)

2. What documents should I bring?

  • Copy of identification with photo (Passport for foreigners)
  • $20 in cash
  • Health insurance card (if available, printed copy)
  • Liability waiver fully completed and signed – unless already signed online

3. What clothes should I bring?

  • Pants for hiking or sports activities (do NOT bring jeans)
  • Sports shorts / swim shorts
  • Short sleeve t-shirts
  • Long sleeve t-shirts
  • Cap or hat
  • Underwear
  • Jacket for low temperatures (preferably waterproof)
  • Sports socks for hiking (approximately 3 pairs)

4. What shoes should I bring?

  • Sneakers or hiking boots

5. What should I bring to sleep?

  • A camping tent (for individual use), recommended for up to 2 people / Example: Amazon link
  • Sleeping bag for low temperatures / Example: Amazon link
  • 2 Emergency thermal blankets (aluminized) / Example: Amazon link
  • Portable mattresses (Yogamat) / Example: Amazon link
    Note: this is not the same as an emergency thermal blanket

6. How will I cook? What should I bring?

  • 1 aluminum mug to boil water / Example: Amazon link
  • 1 plastic spoon and 1 plastic fork (no metal allowed)
  • Plastic water bottle – 32oz
  • Do NOT bring any food

7. Personal hygiene items

  • 2 face towels
  • Wet wipes
  • Band-aids
  • Vaseline, talcum powder or diaper rash ointment
  • Mosquito and tick repellent
  • Sunscreen
  • Toothbrush and Toothpaste
  • Deodorant
  • Other personal hygiene items if necessary

Remember: respect the environment!

MANDATORY ITEMS

Without the items below you will NOT be able to participate in the TOP:

  • 1 rain-resistant camping tent for INDIVIDUAL use
  • 2 emergency / thermal blankets (aluminized) minimum size 2.10m x 1.40m
  • 1 plastic tarp minimum size 6ft x 6ft
  • 1 waterproof jacket or raincoat
  • 1 flashlight
  • Portable mattress (Yogamat)
  • 4 hand and feet warmers ("Hand Warmers - Toe Warmers". E.g. "HotHands")
  • Plastic water bottle, 32oz
  • Snake Gaiters Leg Guards/Snake Chaps (protection against snake bites). Cannot be made of Velcro, must have a protective lock, for example, zipper or quick release
  • Bible (bring it in a container that prevents it from getting wet)
  • In some cases, we may require a medical certificate of health. If this certificate is required, it will become a mandatory item for the participant.
  • Sharp blades and any other type of weapon are prohibited
