WHAT TO PACK?

Follow the Senderista Packing List

Backpack

  • Backpack around 45L capacity
  • Total backpack weight around 14kg (31 lbs)
  • Waterproof backpack cover
  • Your backpack is personal and individual
  • Use ziplock bags to protect documents

Documents

  • Copy of identification with photo
  • $20 in cash
  • Passport (for foreigners)
  • Health insurance card (if available)
  • Signed waiver form

Clothing

  • Hiking pants or sports pants (no jeans)
  • Sports shorts / swim shorts
  • Short sleeve shirts
  • Long sleeve shirt
  • Underwear
  • Hat or cap
  • Cold weather jacket

Personal Items

  • Toothbrush
  • Toothpaste
  • Soap
  • Deodorant
  • Small towel
  • Flashlight or headlamp

Important Notes

  • Prepare your backpack carefully
  • Do not bring unnecessary items
  • Keep backpack organized
  • Follow the instructions from the leaders
